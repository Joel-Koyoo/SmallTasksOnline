TASK STATUS

Tasks go through stages as displayed by the dashboard as shown below

AVAILABLE

This is immediately after you have submitted your task. Informs our team your task is to be done. Task can still be edited

Claimed

This means a member of the team is working on your task and the task is in progress. You cannot edit the task at this stage.

Flagged

This means there is a problem with your task flagged from our team and required your immediate attention

Submitted

This Means your task has been completed and submitted back to you

PAID

This means the task has already been paid for and can now be deleted or in rare cases complained back to us

Under Review

In rare cases, a task may have a problem after submission. The client can complain about the task back to us and this status identifies all tasks with problems from a client
